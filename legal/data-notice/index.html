Data Notice

This Data Notice explains how data is used within the Eanova platform to support its operation, security, and functionality.

Types of Data Processed

Eanova may process basic account details, upload records, activity logs, reward data, and system-related information. This data helps maintain platform operations and user functionality.

Purpose of Data Usage

Data may be used for platform operation, content review, fraud detection, abuse prevention, reward calculation, system improvements, and maintaining service integrity.

Content Review

Uploaded content may be reviewed for originality, policy compliance, safety, and overall usefulness. This ensures fair use and proper functioning of the platform.

Data Retention

Data may be stored for operational, legal, support, analytics, and compliance purposes. Retention periods may vary depending on platform needs and legal requirements.

Security Measures

Eanova takes reasonable steps to protect data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. However, no system can guarantee complete security.

User Acknowledgment

By using the platform, you acknowledge that data may be processed as described in this notice to ensure proper platform operation and service delivery.
