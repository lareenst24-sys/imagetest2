Payment Policy

This Payment Policy explains how rewards, earnings, and payouts are handled on the Eanova platform.

Reward Eligibility

Rewards are not guaranteed for every upload. Earnings may depend on content quality, originality, usefulness, platform review, and compliance with platform rules.

Review and Adjustments

Displayed earnings may be adjusted, delayed, reduced, or removed based on internal review, duplicate detection, policy checks, fraud prevention, or technical verification processes.

Minimum Payout Requirement

Users must reach the required minimum payout threshold before claiming rewards. Payout eligibility depends on account status, activity quality, and platform approval.

Payout Methods

Eanova may offer payout methods such as gift cards or other supported reward options. Availability of payout methods may change over time.

Payout Processing

Payouts may require additional verification and processing time. Delays may occur due to review checks, account verification, or system updates.

Fraud and Abuse

Any attempt to manipulate the system, including spam uploads, duplicate submissions, or fraudulent activity, may result in loss of earnings, payout restrictions, or account suspension.

Policy Changes

Eanova may update payout rules, thresholds, reward systems, and eligibility requirements at any time. Continued use of the platform indicates acceptance of updated policies.
