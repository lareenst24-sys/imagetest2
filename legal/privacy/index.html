Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Eanova collects, uses, and processes information when you use the platform.

Information We Collect

Eanova may collect basic account details such as your name, email address, and login credentials. We may also collect activity data including uploads, usage patterns, and reward-related records.

How We Use Information

Information may be used to operate the platform, provide access to features, manage rewards, prevent abuse, detect fraud, and improve overall system performance.

Content and Activity Data

Uploaded content and related activity may be reviewed for moderation, safety, policy compliance, and platform functionality. This helps maintain platform quality and integrity.

Data Storage

Data may be stored for operational, legal, support, analytics, and compliance purposes as required. We take reasonable steps to protect user data from unauthorized access or misuse.

Sharing of Information

Eanova does not sell personal data. Information may be shared only when required for legal compliance, security purposes, or essential platform operations.

User Responsibility

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account credentials and ensuring that their use of the platform complies with applicable rules and policies.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated over time. Continued use of the platform indicates acceptance of any updated terms.
