Eanova Guide

Help improve AI datasets and earn rewards

Eanova is a creator platform where users can contribute image content that may support future AI dataset improvement and digital training workflows. Rewards may be added based on review, usefulness, quality, policy checks, and platform eligibility.

How Eanova works

Users upload image contributions through the dashboard. Those contributions may be reviewed by the platform for usefulness, originality, policy compliance, and other internal checks. Eligible contributions may help users earn rewards over time.

1

Create your account

Register to access your creator dashboard, profile settings, upload tools, and payout information.

2

Upload image contributions

Use the dashboard upload area to submit images within your available daily limit.

3

Platform review and assessment

Uploads may be assessed for quality, policy compliance, originality, duplication, and usefulness to the platform.

4

Rewards may be added

Reward values are not guaranteed for every upload and may vary depending on review outcomes and internal platform rules.

5

Claim eligible payouts

Eligible users may claim supported rewards such as gift cards, with additional payout options potentially unlocking later.

Important

The amount displayed on the dashboard may not always be final. Additional reward amounts may be added based on assessment and internal review results.

Fair use and platform rules

Spam, duplicate uploads, misleading content, or policy-violating submissions may reduce eligibility for rewards or payout access.
