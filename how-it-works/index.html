Eanova Guide

How Eanova Works

Eanova is a creator platform where users can upload image contributions, track activity, and view reward progress through a secure dashboard. Reward values may vary based on review, usefulness, quality checks, policy compliance, and platform eligibility.

Overview

Users submit image contributions through the dashboard. These contributions may be assessed for originality, usefulness, quality, duplication, and compliance with platform rules. Eligible activity may contribute to reward progress over time.

1

Create your account

Register to access your creator dashboard, profile, upload tools, and reward information.

2

Upload image contributions

Use the dashboard upload area to submit images within your available daily upload limit.

3

Platform review

Contributions may be checked for content quality, originality, duplication, policy compliance, and overall usefulness to the platform.

4

Reward progress updates

Reward progress may increase over time based on contribution activity, bonus activity, internal review outcomes, and platform rules.

5

Claim eligible rewards

Users who meet eligibility requirements and minimum payout thresholds may claim supported reward options such as gift cards.

Important

Earnings and reward values shown on the platform are estimates and may not always be final. Additional review or internal checks may affect displayed amounts.

Platform rules

Duplicate uploads, spam, misleading content, or policy-violating submissions may reduce reward eligibility or limit payout access.

Revenue model

Platform revenue may be supported through advertising and business partnerships. Reward systems may be adjusted over time as the platform develops.
